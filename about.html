Our History

Prelim Formula Pvt Ltd was established in 2018 with an aim to become a one-stop shop for different kinds of solutions. The company started its operations with the human capital solution provider as the first vertical. Taking the baby steps, we have gradually come a long to include more verticals that cater to different customer demands.

Mission
