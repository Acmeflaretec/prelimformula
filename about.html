About Us

Prelim Formula Pvt Ltd, established in 2018, stands as a pioneering consultancy firm offering comprehensive solutions across diverse sectors. Our mission is to be the preferred one-stop solution provider, delivering unparalleled services in human capital, training, tours and travels, logistics, cargo, and infrastructure services globally.

  • Expert Talent Acquisition

    We specialize in sourcing exceptional talent and providing expert workforce management solutions tailored to meet our clients' specific needs.

  • Comprehensive Training Programs

    Our customized training modules cover a wide range of areas including aviation, soft skills, behavioral training, compliance, and technical skills, ensuring our clients' workforce is equipped with the necessary expertise for success.

  • Global Logistics Solutions

    With our logistics and cargo services, we facilitate seamless transportation and delivery solutions, enabling businesses to operate efficiently across borders.

  • Experienced Tours and Travels

    Our tours and travels division offers bespoke travel solutions, ensuring memorable experiences for our clients while meeting their business and leisure travel needs.

  • Robust Infrastructure Services

    We provide comprehensive infrastructure solutions, ranging from airport management to IT sector placements, fostering growth and development across various industries.
